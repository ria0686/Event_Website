Hackathons

CodeSprint 2024

Date: Jan 20, 2024

Location: Technovia University, Main Auditorium

CodeSprint 2024 was a 36-hour non-stop hackathon that brought together over 200 coders, designers, and innovators to tackle real-world tech challenges. Participants developed everything from AI-driven web apps to IoT-based hardware prototypes, attended expert-led workshops, and collaborated with mentors to refine their solutions before pitching to a panel of judges.

BugSmash 2023

Date: Nov 12, 2023

Location: Technovia University, Computer Science Lab

BugSmash 2023 was focused on debugging and optimization. Students gathered to tackle real-world software issues and improve their debugging skills. The event also featured workshops on best practices for writing optimized code, along with interactive problem-solving sessions that helped students refine their programming expertise.

TechFests

Innovate 2023

Date: March 5, 2023

Location: Technovia University, Innovation Center

Innovate 2023 was a showcase of cutting-edge student tech projects, including robots, AI-driven systems, and IoT devices. Students had the opportunity to present their innovative projects to a panel of industry experts, network with professionals, and get valuable feedback. The event also featured live demonstrations and workshops on tech advancements.

VisionTech 2022

Date: Sept 15, 2022

Location: Technovia University, Conference Hall

VisionTech 2022 brought together industry leaders and students for a deep dive into future technologies. The event included seminars on AI, tech quizzes, and live drone exhibitions. It provided students with insights into the future of technology and the opportunities that lie ahead in emerging fields like robotics and machine learning.

Startup Meets

Ideathon Connect 2023

Date: July 10, 2023

Location: Technovia University, Startup Hub

Ideathon Connect 2023 was a networking event where student founders pitched their startup ideas to a panel of mentors, investors, and alumni. The event provided students with valuable feedback, fostered connections with industry professionals, and helped them refine their business models. It was a great opportunity for budding entrepreneurs to gain mentorship and showcase their innovative ideas.

Startup Expo 2022

Date: Dec 2, 2022

Location: Technovia University, Expo Hall

Startup Expo 2022 was a platform for students to exhibit their entrepreneurial ventures. It featured booths showcasing innovative student-led startups, with guidance from successful alumni entrepreneurs. The event included networking sessions, panel discussions, and opportunities for students to pitch their ideas to investors.

Cultural Events

Technovia Fest Night

Date: Feb 18, 2024

Location: Technovia University, Main Auditorium

Technovia Fest Night was an exciting cultural event where students showcased their talents in music, dance, and drama. The event was filled with vibrant performances, including solo and group acts, and was a celebration of the university's diversity. Students enjoyed an evening of entertainment and creative expression.

Ethnic Day

Date: April 6, 2023

Location: Technovia University, Cultural Grounds

Ethnic Day at Technovia was a celebration of India's rich cultural heritage. Students dressed in traditional attire to participate in performances, including dance and music from various regions of India. The event fostered a sense of community, celebrating the diversity of Indian culture while providing a platform for students to showcase their creativity.
