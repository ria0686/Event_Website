Jashn-e-Folk - Traditional Day Celebration

Date: April 13, 2025

Location: Central Plaza

Students wear vibrant ethnic attire and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of India. The event will feature mesmerizing folk dance and music competitions, showcasing traditional art forms like Bhangra, Giddha, and more. Stalls offering regional delicacies and crafts will enhance the festive atmosphere. As part of the Baisakhi celebrations, there will be engaging storytelling sessions about the festival's significance and its importance in Indian history. It's a day to honor traditions, build camaraderie, and revel in the spirit of unity and culture!

LitVerse - Literature & Poetry Festival

Location: Seminar Hall B

A celebration of literature, poetry, and storytelling for writers, poets, and readers alike! The festival features engaging activities such as slam poetry performances, spirited debates, and captivating story slams.
